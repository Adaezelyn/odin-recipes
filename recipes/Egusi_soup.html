Ofe Egusi

Ofe egusi with semo

Ingredients

  • Egusi/li>
  • palm oil
  • fresh pepper
  • Shombo
  • Onions
  • salt
  • seasoning cube
  • Beef
  • crayfish
  • ugu leaf

Steps

  1. Pour a little quantity of water into the pot
  2. mix the grinded Egusi,make moulds and put it into the boiling water
  3. cover to cook for 40 minutes
  4. take out the moulds and pound it
  5. pour in the stock water/li>
  6. Salt, fresh pepper and seasoning cube to taste
  7. put the Beef
  8. close the pot and allow it to cook for 20 minutes
  9. lastly, ugu leaf and Grounded crayfish/li>
