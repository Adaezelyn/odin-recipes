Steps

  1. Pour a little quantity of water into the pot
  2. mix the grinded Ogbono and palm oil in a bowl and pour into the boiling water
  3. Stir continuously so it does burn as it cooks
  4. Add a finely chopped okro, fresh pepper, Shombo and Onions into the mixture
  5. pour in the stock water/li>
  6. Salt and seasoning cube to taste
  7. put the preferred protein
  8. close the pot and allow it to cook for 20 minutes
