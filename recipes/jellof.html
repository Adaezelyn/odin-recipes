Jellof Rice

Nigerian Jellof and Chicken

Ingredients

  • Groundnut oil
  • Onions
  • tomatoes
  • fresh pepper
  • Shombo
  • curry
  • Thyme
  • washed rice
  • seasoning cube

Steps

  1. pour some oil into an empty pot
  2. Add your chopped onions and stir for some time
  3. add the tomatoes,fresh pepper,Shombo curry, Thyme
  4. stir for up to 20 minutes
  5. pour in the stock water
  6. Salt and seasoning cube to taste
  7. close the pot and allow it to cook for 20 minutes
  8. pour the washed rice into the mixture
